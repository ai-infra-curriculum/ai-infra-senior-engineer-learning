# Role Requirements: Senior AI Infrastructure Engineer

**Level:** 2
**Experience Level:** 5-8 years of technical engineering experience, with at least 2-3 years specifically in ML infrastructure or high-performance computing environments
**Market Demand:** Extremely High - Critical shortage of senior talent with deep AI infrastructure expertise. High demand from tech companies scaling AI/LLM infrastructure, particularly for those with GPU optimization and distributed training experience.
**Salary Range (US, 2025):** $150,000 - $280,000 (median $185,000)

## Core Responsibilities
- Lead design and implementation of scalable AI/ML infrastructure solutions
- Architect and deploy GPU-based systems and NVIDIA DGX servers in high-availability environments
- Optimize AI/ML pipelines for performance, cost, and reliability at scale
- Mentor junior engineers and conduct code/architecture reviews
- Drive technical decision-making for infrastructure technology stack
- Design and implement advanced GPU workload management and orchestration
- Build and optimize distributed training infrastructure using Ray, Horovod, or similar frameworks
- Implement advanced monitoring, alerting, and observability solutions
- Lead incident response and post-mortem analysis for critical systems
- Collaborate with ML researchers to translate computational needs into infrastructure requirements
- Establish infrastructure best practices and standards across teams
- Optimize model inference pipelines using TensorRT, ONNX, and quantization techniques
- Design multi-region and multi-cloud deployment strategies
